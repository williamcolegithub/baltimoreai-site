1. Overview

SmartText Assistant ("the Service") is operated by [YOUR NAME] ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our SMS-based assistant service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Phone number: Provided by you during signup, used to send and receive SMS messages.
  • Message content: The text messages you send to and receive from the Service, stored to maintain conversation context.
  • Usage data: Timestamps, message frequency, and feature usage patterns.
  • Preferences: Any personal preferences you share with the Service, such as dietary restrictions, workout goals, or scheduling preferences.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Deliver the Service and respond to your messages
  • Maintain conversation history for context-aware responses
  • Send scheduled reminders and check-ins you have configured
  • Improve the quality and relevance of responses

4. AI Processing

Your messages are processed by AI language models hosted on Amazon Web Services (AWS) to generate responses. Message content is sent to AWS Bedrock for processing and is handled in accordance with AWS's data processing terms. We do not use your messages to train AI models.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on AWS infrastructure in the United States. We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encryption in transit and at rest. Conversation history is retained to provide continuity in the Service and is deleted upon account termination or upon request.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your information may be shared only in the following circumstances:

  • Service providers: AWS processes your data as part of delivering the Service infrastructure.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, regulation, or legal process.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt out: Reply STOP to any message to stop receiving all messages.
  • Request data deletion: Contact us to request deletion of your data.
  • Access your data: Contact us to request a copy of the data we hold about you.

8. Data Retention

We retain your conversation history and personal data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time. Upon account termination, your data will be deleted within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
